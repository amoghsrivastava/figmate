About figmate

figmate helps you work faster with design content - from text transformations to quick access to realistic images and copy.

  • Use Tools for character counting, object counting, and batch renaming with various case styles.
  • Access curated copy content like vendor names and promo tags to make your designs more realistic.
  • Browse and insert high-quality images from curated collections to enhance your mockups.
  • Drag images directly from the plugin onto your canvas or click to insert at your cursor.
